Key Takeaways:

  • justify-content - Aligns items along the MAIN axis (horizontal for row)
  • align-items - Aligns items along the CROSS axis (vertical for row)
  • flex-start - Pack items at the start
  • flex-end - Pack items at the end
  • center - Center items
  • space-between - Space between items, edges touching container
  • space-around - Equal space around each item
  • space-evenly - Equal space everywhere
  • stretch - Stretch items to fill container
